About Us

Meet the experts behind exceptional thatching

Expert Craftsmanship, Authentic Tradition

Kgorogang Thatching Services brings together years of expertise, traditional craftsmanship, and a passion for creating beautiful, lasting thatched structures.

Led by Piet Seboga, our team specializes in high-end thatching for lodges, estates, and premium properties across South Africa. Based in Limpopo, we're mobile and ready to travel to any province to bring your thatching vision to life.

Our commitment goes beyond simply installing a roof – we're preserving an authentic African tradition while using modern techniques to ensure durability, safety, and beauty that lasts for decades.

Lodge Specialist
